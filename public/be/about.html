Making Compliance Effortless

We believe that following labor laws shouldn't be a burden. Our mission is to automate compliance so Norwegian businesses can focus on what matters most.

Our Story

Roaster was born from a simple observation: Norwegian businesses were spending countless hours manually ensuring labor law compliance, often still making costly mistakes.

Our founders, former HR managers who dealt with Arbeidstilsynet inspections firsthand, knew there had to be a better way. They set out to build software that would make compliance automatic, eliminating the stress and uncertainty that comes with manual schedule management.

Today, Roaster serves over 500 Norwegian businesses, from small retail shops to large hospitals, helping them schedule with confidence and stay compliant without the headaches.
