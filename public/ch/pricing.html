Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

What happens after the 14-day free trial?

After your trial ends, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't select one, your account will revert to read-only mode, but your data remains safe for 30 days.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and invoice payments for annual contracts.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans include complimentary onboarding and data migration assistance.

What if I exceed my employee limit?

We'll notify you when you approach your limit. You can upgrade to a higher tier or contact us for custom pricing if you need more flexibility.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first month, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.
