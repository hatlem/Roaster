Everything You Need, Mobile

A full-featured app for employees on the go

View Your Schedule

See your upcoming shifts at a glance. Weekly, monthly, or list view with all the details you need including location, role, and notes.

Shift Swaps

Request to swap shifts with colleagues or post shifts to the marketplace. Get notifications when swaps are approved.

Time-Off Requests

Submit vacation, sick leave, and other time-off requests directly from the app. Track your balance and approval status.

Clock In/Out

Start and end your shifts with a tap. Track your actual hours worked with optional GPS verification for remote locations.

Push Notifications

Stay informed with instant notifications for schedule changes, swap requests, messages, and shift reminders.

Team Chat

Communicate with your team, receive broadcast messages, and coordinate shift coverage all within the app.
