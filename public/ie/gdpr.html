Supporting Data Subject Rights

Roaster makes it easy to fulfill GDPR obligations for your employees

Right to Access

Employees can view all their personal data stored in Roaster through their employee portal. Export functionality available in multiple formats.

Right to Rectification

Incorrect data can be corrected immediately. Employees can request updates through the portal, and managers can approve changes.

Right to Erasure

Complete data deletion when an employee leaves. Automated processes ensure no data remains after the retention period (except where legally required).

Right to Portability

Export employee data in machine-readable formats (JSON, CSV). Easy migration if you ever need to switch systems.

Right to Restrict

Processing can be restricted on request while disputes are resolved. Data remains stored but not processed for scheduling.

Right to Object

Employees can object to specific types of processing. We provide tools to manage consent and objections.

Automated Decisions

AI suggestions are transparent and always require human approval. No fully automated decisions affecting employees.

Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR. Comprehensive incident response.

How We Handle Your Data

Transparent data processing practices

Data We Collect

  • Employee names and contact details
  • Work schedules and shift assignments
  • Hours worked and time records
  • Time-off requests and approvals
  • Scheduling preferences

How We Use It

  • Creating compliant work schedules
  • Validating against labor law requirements
  • Generating compliance reports
  • Sending notifications and reminders
  • Payroll integration (if enabled)

Retention Periods

  • Active data: While employed
  • Compliance records: 2+ years (legal requirement)
  • After termination: 30 days
  • Backups: Up to 90 days
  • Audit logs: 2 years
